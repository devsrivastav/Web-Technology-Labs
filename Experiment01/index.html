Departmental Information

Computer Science

The Computer Science department focuses on the study of computing, programming, and data analysis. Our courses cover various topics including algorithms, artificial intelligence, and software engineering.

Mechanical Engineering

The Mechanical Engineering department offers a comprehensive curriculum that includes mechanics, thermodynamics, and materials science. Students gain hands-on experience through labs and projects.

Electrical Engineering

The Electrical Engineering department emphasizes the study of electrical systems and electronics. Courses include circuit analysis, signal processing, and renewable energy systems.

Civil Engineering

The Civil Engineering department prepares students to design and construct infrastructure projects such as buildings, bridges, and roads. We emphasize sustainable practices and innovative materials.
